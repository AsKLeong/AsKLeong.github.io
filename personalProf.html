About Me

Myers-Briggs: INFP-t (Mediator) Learning Style Test: Visual Mechanical Aptitude Test: 9/10

The test results show that suggest that I learn more from being able to have a concrete idea of what I'm being taught. The Myer-Briggs results also suggest that I'm prone to using metaphors are similes, perhaps this is the result of trying to use already known concepts to explain more abstract notions. When I'm in a team I may need to understand the intent behind what ever task we're accomplishing as the Myer-Briggs result suggest that I am driven more on principles and morals, so I will be more motivated if there is a good goal for my work. The resuts also suggest that I may be strongly affected by interpersonal discord and struggle when needing to interact with a large amount of people. With this knowledge I would suggest that I be placed in a small team who all work well with each other, I would also suggest having a team leader who can focus my attention to specific task as I may be prone to becoming distracting by my own thoughts.
